About Us

We are a rapidly developing fighting game community based in the University of Michigan. We are an official student org, with participation from the student body, alumni, Ann Arbor locals, and even some people from the greater midwest!

Danger Time

Danger time is our weekly event, currently held every monday in mason hall. This casual event welcomes all, newcomers and verterans alike, to test their skills, learn about new games, and hang out with fellow fighting game enthusaists!

Michigan Mixup

Michigan Mixup is our Bi-Monthly large event with attendees from all over the midwest. We come together to play a variety of games, with a substantial turnout of around 100 entries. We even managed to have a 700$ prize pool for one of our more popular games, Melty Blood: Current Code Actress Again

Michigan Mixup 5v5

A major part of the michigan mix up is a livestreamed 5v5 exhibition match between UMICH students and MSU students. The game of choice is Guilty Gear Strive, with MSU currently holding the title.
